Read the current `wiki/` and `raw/papers/` content.

Produce a focused research gap report in `gaps/research_gaps.md` with these sections:
- High-confidence gaps
- Novelty delta versus nearest-neighbor work
- Conflicting claims
- Counterexamples or falsification tests to run next
- Missing baselines or ablations
- Bad-case slices worth isolating
- Prune or continue rules
- Datasets or benchmarks to validate next
- Concrete next search queries

Requirements:
- Be specific and cite paper ids or wiki page names.
- Do not only praise ideas; state why a claim could fail.
- For each promising gap, write what evidence would support it and what evidence would kill it.
- When the novelty delta is weak or unclear, say so directly.
- When a direction should be paused unless a specific condition is met, write that prune condition explicitly.
